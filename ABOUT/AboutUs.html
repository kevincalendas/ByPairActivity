ABOUT US

Flop Quiz is a fun and interactive web-based quiz application designed to test your knowledge and speed. With its charming pixel-art interface and smooth gameplay, it offers a delightful experience for students and trivia lovers alike.

The quiz consists of 10 challenging questions. Do you have what it takes to get a perfect score, or will you end up as a "flop"? Only those with the sharpest minds will survive this round.
